## Developer Implementation Requirements

### Accessibility & SEO Standards

1. All alt-text must be semantically anchored to the **Internal Legal System** metaphor framework.
2. Alt-text length should be 100–160 characters where possible for optimal screen reader pacing.
3. Images must maintain WCAG AA contrast minimums when overlaid with captions.

### Visual Pacing Protocol

1. Maintain **6pt line spacing** between images and their respective **Bolded Thematic Anchors** to preserve the "grounded" aesthetic.
2. Images must load progressively—use lazy loading for images below the fold to respect bandwidth for neurodivergent users with sensory sensitivities.
3. Hover states must be subtle (4px translateY) to avoid visual overstimulation.

### File Format & Optimization

1. All images: JPG format, optimized for web (max 200KB per file).
2. Aspect ratio: 4:3 for gallery cards to maintain visual consistency.
3. Color grading: Warm, muted tones aligned with the Forest Green (#1B4332) and Burnished Gold (#C9963A) palette.
